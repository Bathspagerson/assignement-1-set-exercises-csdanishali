Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian pasta dish combines eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper for a creamy, delicious meal that's simple yet satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Method

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy in a pan.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and quickly toss with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and add egg mixture, stirring fast to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve hot.
