Plot

*Stranger Things* begins in the quiet town of Hawkins, Indiana, in the 1980s. When a young boy named Will Byers mysteriously vanishes, his friends and family begin a desperate search. They encounter a girl with strange abilities, uncover a secret government experiment, and discover an alternate dimension called the Upside Down. As the mystery deepens, the group faces horrifying creatures and secrets that threaten their world.
